Frequently Asked Questions

1. What size costume does my large dog need?

Measure your dog’s chest girth, neck circumference, and back length. Most large breeds need XL or 2XL, while giants like Newfoundlands may need 3XL.

2. Are dog costumes safe for large breeds?

Yes, as long as they’re breathable, adjustable, and don’t restrict movement. Avoid costumes with choking hazards or tight elastics.

3. What are the most popular large dog costume ideas?

Chewbacca, lion, NFL jerseys, and dinosaur costumes are top picks. Shark and bat costumes are gaining popularity too!

4. How long can my dog wear a costume?

Limit wear time to 30–60 minutes and monitor for any signs of discomfort or overheating.

5. Can I reuse my dog’s costume?

Absolutely! Most quality dog costumes can be reused for parties, photoshoots, or next year’s Halloween.
